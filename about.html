Built on Vision

Established in 1990, ICB was founded with an unwavering dedication to delivering comprehensive construction management services that contribute to the betterment of our community. Fueled by a passion for excellence and a mission to elevate the places we call home, our foundation rests on decades of industry knowledge and expertise. ICB has successfully completed over $600 million worth of commercial, municipal, religious, and educational construction projects across Georgia. By continually implementing best practices, ensuring successful timely delivery, and fostering continued growth, ICB has solidified its position as a leader in Georgia’s construction industry.

People Building People

Building Partnerships + Uplifting Communities

At ICB Construction Group, our purpose is clear: to establish enduring partnerships with clients, rooted in trust. We’re driven by a commitment to give back to the communities we call home, creating positive impacts through “community uplift” initiatives. By focusing on socially beneficial projects like schools, parks, and libraries, we leverage our expertise to bring about meaningful change.

Our Process

Listen

The foundation of a successful project lies in truly understanding our clients’ needs and goals, and the best way to receive this information is by forging an open, honest, and trusting relationship with them. To do this, we begin by listening intently, ensuring we grasp the unique vision and requirements of each project.

Build

With meticulous attention to detail, skilled craftsmanship, and a commitment to excellence, we transform your vision into reality, building structures that exceed expectations and stand the test of time.

Learn

Our team of experts delves deep into the project details, conducting thorough research and analysis to gain a comprehensive understanding of the challenges, opportunities, and optimal strategies for success.

Time to Talk

We look forward to learning about you and your project.
